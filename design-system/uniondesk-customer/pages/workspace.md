# Workspace Override

## Page Intent

- Customer workspace home for ticket overview, quick submit, ticket classification, and latest notices.
- Keep it operational and compact, not editorial.

## Required Structure

- First row: 4 summary cards
  - 业务域总数
  - 工单总数
  - 已处理
  - 未处理
- Left column:
  - 工单分类统计
  - 快捷访问菜单
- Right column:
  - 公告与通知卡片列表

## Layout Rules

- The right announcement column must keep the same visual height as the left stack when no data exists.
- Empty state must not collapse the right column.
- Quick access tiles should stay small and functional.

## Visual Rules

- Use support-portal colors from the Master file.
- Keep cards compact, rounded, and readable.
- Avoid dashboard decoration that feels like a promotional landing page.

## Content Rules

- Use support-oriented labels only.
- Do not introduce marketing copy, event language, or generic product slogans.
