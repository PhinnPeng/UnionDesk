# Portal Shell Override

## Page Intent

- The shell for the customer support portal.
- It should support working across workspace, domains, inbox, ticket detail, and settings.

## Required Structure

- Left navigation with clear active states
- Top bar with status, current domain, and session actions
- Responsive collapse for mobile and tablet

## Visual Rules

- Keep navigation and content surfaces consistent with the Master palette.
- No public landing hero, no marketing CTA stack, no decorative promo blocks.

## Interaction Rules

- Active route highlighting must be obvious.
- Keep mobile controls compact and accessible.
